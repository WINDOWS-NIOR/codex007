Our Accommodation Services

Comfort and Convenience in Every Location

NIOR GROUP's Accommodation Services division provides comprehensive housing solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of corporate clients, expatriates, and long-term residents. We offer a variety of comfortable and well-maintained properties, coupled with exceptional management services that ensure a hassle-free living experience.

Our portfolio includes a wide range of housing options across different locations, catering to various budget requirements and preferences. Each property is carefully selected and maintained to meet our strict quality standards, ensuring comfortable and secure accommodation for all our clients.

Corporate Housing

Specially designed accommodations for businesses needing to house employees for short to long-term assignments.

Residential Complexes

Fully-managed residential complexes offering comfortable living spaces with community amenities and security.

Short-term Rentals

Flexible accommodation options for temporary stays, project-based assignments, or transitional periods.

Property Management

End-to-end property management services including maintenance, tenant relations, and administrative support.
